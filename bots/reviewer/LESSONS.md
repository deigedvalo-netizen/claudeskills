## Candidates (bot-proposed; a human moves lines up to Approved)
- When a "success/positive" button variant is added outside the primary token pairing, re-check its text/background contrast independently — inherited white-on-brand-color rarely survives on mid-luminance greens.
- A single muted-text token reused across base + raised + sunken surfaces will pass on the darkest ground and quietly fail on the lighter ones; verify the token against every surface it actually lands on, not just the page background.
- "Tokens identical in both themes" proves parity but not sufficiency — still spot-compute AA on the real foreground/background pairings.
